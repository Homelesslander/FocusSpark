About this project

A small, friendly web app designed to help Nuerodivergent persons manage tasks through gentle gamification and clear visual progress.

Why this exists

Neurodiversity describes the idea that people experience and interact with the world around them in many ways. There is no right nor wrong way of thinking, learning, and behaving, and differences are not deficits, but neurodiversity is often used in the context of autism spectrum disorder (ASD), as well as other neurological or developmental conditions such as ADHD or learning disabilities. These Neurodevelopmental conditions can cause impairment and create obstacles that get in the way of neurodiverse people doing the things they want to do. This Webpage applies a Positive Behavior Support(PBS) framework, to help encourage adolescents to follow through with daily task completion and management of their own schedules.
