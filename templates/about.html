About this project

A small, friendly web app designed to help people with ADHD manage tasks through gentle gamification and clear visual progress.

Why this exists

Many adolescents with ADHD struggle with starting tasks, keeping track of responsibilities, and staying motivated. Traditional task managers can feel overwhelming and fail to provide the encouragement that helps form habits. This project applies simple game mechanics — points, streaks and progress indicators — to make completing tasks clearer and more rewarding.

Simple task lists

Quickly add tasks, set importance, and see what matters most today.

Progress tracking

Earn points and watch your progress increase — small wins add up.

Safe and private

Minimal personal information required. You control your tasks and points.
