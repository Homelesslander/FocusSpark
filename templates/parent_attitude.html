📋 Parent Recommendations

  • For Positive Attitudes: Celebrate their achievements and maintain consistent routines. Consider offering extra privileges or praise.
  • For Neutral/Okay Attitudes: Check in about their day and offer support. Sometimes a break or change of scenery can help improve mood.
  • For Challenging Attitudes: Practice patience and understanding. Consider if they need more support with tasks, are feeling overwhelmed, or may need professional help.
  • For Low Energy: Ensure they're getting enough sleep and nutrition. Break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps.
  • General Tips: Maintain open communication, validate their feelings, and work together on solutions. Remember that attitude fluctuations are normal.
